Heart Disease Classification

This study focuses on developing machine learning classification models for the accurate identification of individuals at risk of developing heart disease. The research utilizes the CDC's BRFSS 2021 dataset. Logistic Regression, Naive Bayes, Random Forest, and XGBoost are employed for this project. The goal is to optimize for balanced accuracy and select a model with high recall, ensuring frequent identification of individuals who are sick. By leveraging relevant health and demographic data, the developed model assists healthcare professionals in targeting preventive measures, enhancing patient outcomes, and reducing the overall burden of heart disease on the healthcare system.
